# What's in the game:
FeatureDetails 
- Player - Red square, moves with WASD
- Shoot - Hold LMB — rapid-fire yellow bullets toward cursor
- Dash - Press Space — quick directional burst (1.2s cooldown), grants i-frames
- Cross Blast - RMB — 8 bullets explode outward from the click point (3s cooldown)
- Dummy enemies 6 grey squares standing still, take damage and die
- Rat - Blue square that walks toward you and damages on contact
- Collision - Bullets hit enemies; rat contact damages player with i-frames
- Particles - Hit sparks, dash trail, death bursts
- Screen shake - Triggers on hits, deaths, cross blast
- HUD - HP bar, dash/cross cooldown bars, score, crosshair cursor

## situação maneira
- vc derrotou um boss goblin mago e ele dropou a cabeça dele e vc pode vestir ela pra ganhar bonus de status e uma magia nova
- um inimigo dropou umas pernas que te ajudam a dashar mais longe, menos custo de mana com abilidades de movimento
- inimigo snake (começa peqeno, vai comendo e ficando grande, previnir dele feixar o level), tem qe bater na cabeça dele
